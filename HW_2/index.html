About Me

I recently graduated from Elmhurst College; did not play sports there but I did support my fellow Bluejays! My preferred method of exercise is cycling on my peloton; if you peloton, you should follow me and hold me accountable!

To relax, I enjoy watching The Office or reading; currently reading Outliers. I also enjoy visiting new restaurants and breweries. I worked for Lagunitas Brewing Company for 5 years. They make my favorite beer but I am always willing to try something new.

I am extremely excited for this Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp and hoping to transition into a role as a Web Developer in 2020.
